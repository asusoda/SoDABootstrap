Sponsoring SoDA annually:

SoDA offers many different annual sponsorship packages.

○ Gold Tier - $4000

■ Listed as gold sponsor on website and monthly newsletter

■ Logo on annual SoDA tablecloth

■ Bound hard copy, in addition to electronic copy, of the SoDA resume book

■ Opportunity to organize a trip for some of our members to your local company office

■ Opportunity to set up a table at the annual SoDA coding competition in the spring

■ Receive gold-tier perks for SoDA’s coding competitions

■ Receive all perks encompassed in the Silver and Bronze sponsorship Tiers

○ Silver Tier - $2500

■ Listed as silver sponsor on website and monthly newsletter

■ Acknowledge sponsorship on social media

■ Opportunity to provide us with promotional material to distribute in our newsletter or at club meetings

■ Receive silver-tier perks for SoDA’s coding competitions

■ Receive all perks encompassed in the Bronze sponsorship Tier

○ Bronze Tier - $1000

■ Listed as bronze sponsor on website and monthly newsletter

■ Logo on back of annual and travel SoDA club t-shirts

■ Preference when scheduling events during the school year

■ Access to electronic version of member resume book*

* The electronic version of our member resume book is available upon request for non-sponsors.

If your company or organization is interested in sponsoring SoDA, send an email to industry.asu@thesoda.io to contact our Industry Outreach Co-Chairs. They will help you through the process and answer any questions you may have.

Sponsoring SoDA events:

In addition to our annual sponsorship options, SoDA offers event-specific sponsorships. We hold many events throughout fall and spring semesters, many of which are great sponsorhip opportunities. To learn more about our specific events and our event specific sponsorship packages please send an email to industry.asu@thesoda.io. Our Industry Outreach Chair will be able to outline the program and answer any questions you may have about it.
