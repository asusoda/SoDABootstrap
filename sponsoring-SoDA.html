Sponsoring SoDA annually:

SoDA offers many different annual sponsorship packages.

○ Gold Tier - $4000

■ Listed as gold sponsor on our club website

■ Name on back of annual and travel SoDA club t-shirts

■ Bound hard copy, in addition to electronic copy, of the SoDA resume book

■ Listed as gold sponsor in monthly SoDA newsletter

■ Logo on annual SoDA banner

■ Opportunity to provide us with promotional material to distribute after meetings

■ Opportunity to organize a trip for some of our members to your local office

■ Opportunity to set up a recruiting stand at the annual SoDA coding competition in the spring

■ Receive gold-tier perks for SoDA’s coding competitions

■ Receive all perks encompassed in the Silver and Bronze sponsorship

○ Silver Tier - $2500

■ Listed as silver sponsor on our club website

■ Listed as silver sponsor in monthly SoDA newsletter

■ Acknowledgment of sponsorship on social media

■ Receive silver-tier perks for SoDA’s coding competitions

■ Receive all perks encompassed in the Bronze sponsorship Tier

○ Bronze Tier - $1000

■ Listed as bronze sponsor on our club website

■ Access to electronic version of the SoDA resume book

■ Listed as bronze sponsor in newsletter

■ Logo on back of annual and travel SoDA club t-shirts

■ Opportunity to receive the two quarterly Mentor Progress Summaries

■ Opportunity to upgrade to a higher sponsorship tier for the cost of the difference

■ Opportunity to upgrade sponsorship tier at specific SoDA events for the cost of the difference

* The electronic version of our member resume book is available upon request for non-sponsors.

If your company or organization is interested in sponsoring SoDA, send an email to industry.soda@asu.edu to contact our Industry Outreach Co-Chairs. They will help you through the process and answer any questions you may have.

Sponsoring SoDA events:

In addition to our annual sponsorship options, SoDA offers event-specific sponsorships. We hold many events throughout fall and spring semesters, many of which are great sponsorhip opportunities. To learn more about our specific events and our event specific sponsorship packages please send an email to industry.soda@gmail.com. Our Industry Outreach Co-Chairs will be able to outline the program and answer any questions you may have about it.
